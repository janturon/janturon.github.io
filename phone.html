Phone number input

  • allow + at the beginning
  • otherwise allow only numbers and spaces
  • add space after sequence of 3 numbers
  • minimize autospaces count
  • allow space after 1 or 2 numbers, too
  • replace sequence of spaces by single space
  • don't allow space after +
  • keep cursor position if replacement happens

Consider pattern attribute to enforce specific format.

<input oninput="phone(this)" placeholder="Your phone number..."> <script> const phone = (o,p) => ( p=o.selectionStart, o.value = o.value .replace(/(^\+)|[^0-9 ]|\s+(?=\s)/g, '$1') .replace(/(\d)(\d{2})(?=\S)/g, (_,p1,p2,q) => p==q+1 ? p1+' '+p2 : (p++,p1+p2+' ')), o.setSelectionRange(p,p) ) </script>
